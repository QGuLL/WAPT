A private (.pem) - public (.crt) keypair will be generated to allow for package authentication.
Only packages signed with your private key may be installed onto agents.
The private key (.pem) must not be disclosed to a third party under any circumstances.
The public certificate (.crt) may however be disclosed. It will be deployed onto client hosts via the custom installer.

NB. : the private key must be generated only once per organisation.
For further information : Generating a self-signed certificate.
